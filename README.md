## What the Application Assesses

QACI looks for applicants who demonstrate:

- Academic ability across a breadth of subjects
- A high level of commitment and application to study
- High standards of personal behaviour
- Commitment to personal and social development
- A sense of citizenship — care and concern for others

## Checklist

**Documents needed**
- [ ] Passport-style photo — school ID or passport-style, taken within last 2 years (grab tomorrow morning in uniform)
- [ ] Identity & citizenship evidence — birth certificate + passport (and visa if applicable)
  - [ ] Birth certificate — may need to be physical original
  - [ ] Passport
- [ ] 2 most recent semester school reports
- [ ] NAPLAN results — Year 3, 5, 7, and 9 (Year 9 may be requested once available)
- [ ] Evidence of giftedness (if available) — formal assessment or report from qualified professional, school Guidance Officer, or Gifted Education Coordinator; requires written consent to share
- [ ] Medical documentation (AARA only) — only if seeking Access Arrangements; diagnosis letter on official letterhead, no more than 3 years old
